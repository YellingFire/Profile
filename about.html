My Name is Chad Chadwick

I was born in Albuqurque, New Mexico and lived there until I was six. We moved to southern California for a brief time and moved to Colorado by 3rd grade. I have lived in Colorado since. I attended Highschool in Aurora at Eaglecrest HS, was an AllState Football and Baseball player and went to College at Mesa State University and Metro in downtown Denver.

Out of college I started a Construction company called "Chadwicks Construction" I stayed in that field for almost 17 years. The housing and construction market tanked in 2008 forcing a change. I started a wholesale coffee roasting company (Java Rush Specialty Coffee Roasters) I am the current owner/operator of the company. In the course of running the coffee business, I found that I could complete the work needed to run the company on a part time basis. I then decided to leverage the knowledge that I gained in my construction years, and started a Home Inspection Business which I also currently own and operate, (Seven Stars Home Advisors) Along the way I taught myself how to utilize Wordpress to build and maintain my businesses websites. I built both websites linked above.

Outside of work, I have two kids, a boy (10yo) and girl (6yo) and a wonderful wife. We have a camper and enjoy camping, fishing, hiking and hunting in the wonderful state we live in.
